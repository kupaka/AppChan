AppChan by zixaphir

Appify 4chan!

A 4chan userstyle with a focus on 4chan x integration.

Dependencies

Firefox with Stylish OR Scriptish Chrome as a userscript OR with Stylish Opera as userjs

Install

In a perfect world, it'd be as simple as... Go here: http://userstyles.org/styles/54149/appchan Install But that may not be the case.
